> Steps to set up a Webhook in PayU Dashboard

Follow these steps to configure webhooks for successful payments, failed payments, and refunds in the PayU dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access PayU Dashboard">
    Log in to your PayU Dashboard using your credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Webhook Settings">
    - Go to Settings in the dashboard.
    - Select Webhooks from the available options.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a Webhook for Successful Payments">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Click on the **Create Webhook** button.
    - In the event type, select **Payments – Successful**.
    - In the webhook URL field, paste the following URL:
    ```
    https://api.flexype.in/v1/public/pg/payu/webhook
    ```
    - Click **Save** to finalize the webhook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Webhook for Failed Payments">
    - Click on the **Create Webhook** button.
    - In the event type, select **Payments – Failed**.
    - In the webhook URL field, paste the following URL:
    ```
    https://api.flexype.in/v1/public/pg/payu/webhook
    ```
    - Click **Save** to finalize the webhook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Webhook for Refunds">
    - Click on the **Create Webhook** button.
    - In the event type, select **Refunds**.
    - In the webhook URL field, paste the following URL:
    ```
    https://api.flexype.in/v1/public/pg/payu/webhook
    ```
    - Click **Save** to finalize the webhook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retrieve and Configure API Key">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Go to the [Payments](https://dashboard.flexype.io/settings/payment) in the FlexyPe dashboard.
    - Copy the **Key** and **Salt** from the PayU Dashboard and paste them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Webhooks (If Needed)">
    If any modifications are required, click on the "Edit" button next to the webhook.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>Do not delete the credentials if the payment provider is in use</Warning>
