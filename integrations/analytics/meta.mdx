> Steps to integrate Meta Pixels in FlexyPe Dashboard

Setting up your Meta integration with FlexyPe is straightforward. Just follow these steps to seamlessly track conversions and optimize your campaigns.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Meta Business Suite">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your Meta Business Suite.
    - Click on the **All Tools** menu and select **Events Manager**.
    - Navigate to **Data Sources**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Shopify Pixel">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Choose the Shopify pixel for which you want to track conversions.
    - Copy the **Pixel ID**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate with FlexyPe">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Go to [Meta Pixels](https://dashboard.flexype.io/settings/integrations/meta) in the FlexyPe dashboard.
    - Click on the **Add pixel** button.
    - Paste the **Pixel ID** into the designated field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and Add Your API Token">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Return to your Meta Business Suite and open the pixel you're using.
    - Go to the Settings tab for that pixel.
    - Scroll down to **Conversions API** and click on **Generate Access Token**.
    - Copy the generated **API token**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize the Integration in FlexyPe">
    - Head back to your FlexyPe Dashboard.
    - Paste the **API token** you copied and turn on the API Status.
    - Click **Save** to finalize the Meta Pixel.
  </Step>
</Steps>
