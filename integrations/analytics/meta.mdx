title: 'Meta Pixels'
---

Integrating Meta with FlexyPe is just as straightforward. Follow a few steps to track key conversion events like purchases on your website. This allows you to accurately measure campaign performance and optimize your ads, audiences, and strategies using data-driven insights.

## Getting the API keys and Pixel ID

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing your Meta Business Suite.">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-1.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your [Meta Business Suite](https://business.facebook.com).
    - Click on the All Tools menu and select Events Manager.
    - Navigate to Data Sources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your Shopify Pixel">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-2.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - From the list of available pixels, locate the one linked to your Shopify store.
    - Select the pixel to connect it with your conversion tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Getting the Pixel ID">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-3.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Navigate to the Settings tab for the selected Shopify pixel.
    - Copy the **Dataset ID** and keep it handy for configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generating and Getting your Conversion API">
     <img
        className="rounded-lg"
        src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-4.png"
        alt="Hero Dark"
     />
    - On the same Settings tab scroll down to Conversions API.
    - Select **Conversion API Gateway**.
    - Click on Generate Access Token if it hasn't been generated yet.
    - Copy the generated token and save it securely, as you’ll need it for configuration.
    <Warning>Do not share this token — it provides access to your conversion data</Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integrate Meta Pixel with FlexyPe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Meta Pixels Integration on your FlexyPe Dashboard.">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-5.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your [FlexyPe Dashboard](https://dashboard.flexype.io).
    - Click on the Settings menu and select Integrations.
    - Navigate to Meta Pixels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure a new Pixel">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-6.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Click on the Add pixel button.
    - Paste the copied **Dataset ID** into the pixel id field.
    - Paste the copied **Conversion API** into the designated field and turn on the API Status.
    - Click Save to finalize the Pixel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish Setup and Start Tracking">
   <img
     className="rounded-lg"
     src="/images/integrations/analytics/mp-7.png"
     alt="Hero Dark"
   />
   - Once you've added the Pixel, your setup is complete.
   - FlexyPe is now connected to your Shopify store and ready to track conversions from Meta Pixel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you’ll successfully integrate Meta with FlexyPe, enabling seamless conversion tracking and enhanced campaign optimization.
