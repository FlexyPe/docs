> Steps to integrate Google Analytics in FlexyPe Dashboard

FlexyPe makes it incredibly easy to track checkout events on Google Analytics 4 (GA4) through a seamless backend API integration. Once you've configured the settings on your FlexyPe Dashboard, these events will automatically start appearing in your GA4 funnel under **Reports** → **Engagement** → **Events**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Conversions in Google Analytics">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your Google Analytics account.
    - Simply type **Measurement ID** into the search bar and copy the Measurement ID that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate with FlexyPe">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Go to [Google Analytics](https://dashboard.flexype.io/settings/integrations/google-analytics) in the FlexyPe dashboard.
    - Click on the **Add analytics credential** button.
    - Paste the **Measurement ID** & **API Secret** into the designated field.
    - Click **Save** to finalize the Conversion Action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these straight forward steps, you’ll have a robust setup for tracking checkout events in GA4, giving you valuable insights into your customers' journeys on the FlexyPe platform.
