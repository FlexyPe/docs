title: 'Google Analytics'

Integrating Google Analytics with FlexyPe is quick and easy. Follow a few simple steps to track user behavior and conversion events across your website. With this integration, you gain valuable insights into how customers interact with your checkout, enabling you to optimize your user experience and make informed, data-driven marketing decisions.

## Getting the Measurement ID and API Secret from Google Analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing the Google Analytics dashboard.">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-1.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web).
    - Click on Goals on the left panel and select Events Manager.
    - Navigate to Summary under Conversion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your conversion event">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-2.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Select your Purchase conversion event.
    - On the Details tab scroll down to Tag Setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the Measurement ID">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-3.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Expand the Tag Setup dropdown and select Use Google Tag Manager
    - Copy the **Conversion ID** and **Conversion Label** and keep it handy for configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get its Secret Value">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-4.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Expand the Tag Setup dropdown and select Use Google Tag Manager
    - Copy the **Conversion ID** and **Conversion Label** and keep it handy for configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integrate Google Ads with FlexyPe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Google Analytics Integration on your FlexyPe Dashboard.">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-5.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your [FlexyPe Dashboard](https://dashboard.flexype.io).
    - Click on the Settings menu and select Integrations.
    - Navigate to Google Analytics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure a new conversion action">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-6.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Click on the Add new conversion action.
    - Paste the copied **Measurement ID** into the designated field.
    - Paste the copied **Secret Value** into the API Secret field.
    - Click Save to finalize the conversion action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish Setup and Start Tracking">
   <img
     className="rounded-lg"
     src="/images/integrations/analytics/ga4-7.png"
     alt="Hero Dark"
   />
   - Once you've added the conversion action, your setup is complete.
   - FlexyPe is now connected to your Shopify store and ready to track conversions from Google Analytics.
  </Step>
</Steps>
