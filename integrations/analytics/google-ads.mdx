> Steps to integrate Google Ads in FlexyPe Dashboard

Integrating Google Ads with FlexyPe enables you to track key events on your website and optimize your ads, keywords, ad groups, and campaigns based on these conversion events. By adding conversion tags to the FlexyPe checkout, you can effortlessly track purchase conversions and make data-driven decisions for your marketing strategy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Conversions in Google Ads">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Log in to your Google Ads account.
    - Navigate to **Tools & Settings** and click on **Conversions** under the Measurements column.
    - Select your **Purchase** conversion event and click on the associated conversion action.
    - Under Tag setup, click on **Install the tag yourself**
    - Copy the Conversion ID and label from the snippet here ◦ Conversion ID: AW-321XXX235 ◦ Label: vSRhCPnGs-XXXuSrpkB
    <Note>Conversion API token is known as Label</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate with FlexyPe">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/dashboard.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Go to [Google Ads](https://dashboard.flexype.io/settings/integrations/google-ads) in the FlexyPe dashboard.
    - Click on the **Add conversion action** button.
    - Paste the **Conversion ID** & **Conversion API Token** into the designated field.
    - Click **Save** to finalize the Conversion Action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

With these steps completed, your Google Ads account will be seamlessly integrated with FlexyPe, allowing you to effectively track and optimize your purchase conversion events. This setup not only enhances your advertising strategy but also ensures you’re making the most out of every marketing dollar.
