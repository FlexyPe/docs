## Key Features Across All Coupons

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Flexible Triggering Options">
    Choose between **coupon codes** (customer enters at checkout) or **automatic discounts** (applies automatically when conditions are met). Mix both approaches across different campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Advanced Conditions">
    Set precise requirements based on:
    - **Purchase Amount:** Minimum or maximum cart value
    - **Purchase Quantity:** Number of items in cart
    - **Product/Collection Scope:** Apply to entire store, specific products, or collections
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customer Targeting">
    Control who can use your coupons:
    - **All users** or **specific customers only**
    - **First-time buyers** or **repeat customers**
    - **Order count requirements** for loyalty rewards
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Usage Controls">
    Protect your margins with:
    - **Global usage limits:** Total redemptions across all customers
    - **Per-customer limits:** Prevent coupon abuse
    - **Active periods:** Schedule start and end dates
    - **Discount caps:** Set maximum discount values
    - **COD restrictions:** Disable Cash on Delivery for specific offers
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stackable Discounts">
    Decide whether coupons can be combined with other active promotions. Create complex promotional strategies with multiple discounts working together.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Custom Presentation">
    Control how coupons appear to customers:
    - Show or hide during checkout
    - Add custom titles and descriptions
    - Make offers clear and compelling
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Use Cases

### Boost Average Order Value
- Set minimum purchase requirements for discounts
- Offer free shipping above certain cart values
- Create bundle deals for higher-value purchases

### Drive First Purchases
- Target first-time buyers with exclusive offers
- Combine with email campaigns for abandoned carts
- Remove friction with automatic discounts

### Build Customer Loyalty
- Use purchase-driven coupons to reward repeat customers
- Create VIP discounts for high-value customers
- Offer exclusive gifts to loyal shoppers

### Clear Inventory
- Apply discounts to specific products or collections
- Create Buy X Get Y offers for slow-moving items
- Run limited-time flash sales with usage limits

### Seasonal Campaigns
- Schedule coupons for specific date ranges
- Stack multiple offers during peak seasons
- Create urgency with countdown timers and limited uses

## Best Practices

<Warning>
**Protect Your Margins:** Always set maximum discount caps and usage limits. Monitor coupon performance regularly to ensure promotions remain profitable.
</Warning>

<Tip>
**Test Before Launch:** Use specific user targeting to test coupons with a small group before rolling out store-wide. This helps identify issues and optimize settings.
</Tip>

<Info>
**Clear Communication:** Add descriptive titles and details to every coupon. Customers should immediately understand what they're getting and any requirements.
</Info>

## Getting Started

1. **Choose Your Coupon Type:** Select the promotion that best fits your campaign goals
2. **Configure Settings:** Set up discount values, conditions, and targeting rules
3. **Set Usage Limits:** Protect your business with appropriate restrictions
4. **Customize Presentation:** Make the offer clear and appealing to customers
5. **Monitor Performance:** Track redemptions and adjust settings as needed
