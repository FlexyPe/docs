## Step 1: Setup

Configure how free shipping applies at checkout in **Setup** — choose whether it activates automatically or requires a coupon code.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/FreeShipping/image.png"
  alt="Setup section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Application Type:**  
  - *Coupon Code* – Customer enters a code (e.g., `FREEDEL`).  
  - *Automatic Discount* – Applies automatically when conditions are met.  
- **Shipping Rate Filters:** Exclude high-cost options by setting a maximum eligible rate (e.g., free shipping for rates below ₹200).  

---

## Step 2: Condition

Set **Condition** rules to define when free shipping applies.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/FreeShipping/image1.png"
  alt="Condition section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **No Requirement:** Apply free shipping to all orders.  
- **Minimum/Maximum Purchase Amount:** Require a threshold to qualify.  
- **Minimum/Maximum Quantity:** Apply only when the cart meets specific item counts.  

---

## Step 3: User Eligibility

Control who receives the offer with **User Eligibility** settings.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/FreeShipping/image2.png"
  alt="User eligibility section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **All Users:** Available to everyone.  
- **Specific Users:** Restrict to selected customer groups.  
- **Order Scope:**  
  - *All orders*  
  - *First order only*  
  - *Based on total order history*  

---

## Step 4: Usage Limits

Set redemption controls and time windows under **Usage Limits**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/FreeShipping/image3.png"
  alt="Usage limits section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Global Limit:** Maximum total redemptions allowed.  
- **Per-Customer Limit:** Restrict individual usage.  
- **Stacking Permissions:** Choose whether to combine with other discounts.  
- **Active Window:** Define start and end dates/times for validity.  
- **Disable COD:** Optionally restrict Cash on Delivery when free shipping is active.  

---

## Step 5: Preview

Customize the user-facing display under **Preview**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/FreeShipping/image4.png"
  alt="Preview section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Coupon Visibility:** Show or hide during checkout.  
- **Display Details:** Add descriptive titles such as *“Free Shipping on Orders Above ₹999.”*

## Strategic Value

The **Free Shipping Discount** is one of the most effective ways to:  
- Increase conversion rates by eliminating checkout friction.  
- Encourage higher cart values through minimum spend thresholds.  
- Reward loyal or first-time customers with a smooth purchasing experience.  

FlexyPe’s advanced configuration lets you deploy free shipping confidently — maximizing conversions while maintaining profitability.
