## Step 1: Setup

Configure how the **Purchase Driven Coupon** applies and what value it offers in **Setup**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/PurchaseDriven/image.png"
  alt="Setup section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Application Type:**  
  - *Coupon Code* – Customers enter a code at checkout.  
  - *Automatic Discount* – Applies automatically once conditions are met.  
- **Discount Value:**  
  - *Percentage Off* – e.g., 5% off the total.  
  - *Fixed Amount Off* – e.g., ₹500 off per order.  
- **Maximum Cap:** Set an upper limit on the total discount amount.  

---

## Step 2: User Behavior

Define behavioral triggers in **User Behavior** to target customers based on their past activity.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/PurchaseDriven/image1.png"
  alt="User behavior section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Behavior Period:** Choose a date range to consider customer activity (past X days/weeks/months).  
- **Behavioral Conditions:**  
  - *Used Specific Coupon* – e.g., customers who previously redeemed `WELCOME10`.  
  - *Minimum Total Spend* – e.g., total lifetime purchases above ₹10,000.  
  - *Minimum Number of Orders* – e.g., at least 3 past purchases.  

This targeting ensures that only qualified, engaged customers benefit from the promotion.  

---

## Step 3: Condition

Set **Condition** parameters to define current purchase requirements for applying the discount.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/PurchaseDriven/image2.png"
  alt="Condition section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **No Requirement:** Apply to any qualifying customer.  
- **Min/Max Purchase Value:** Restrict based on order amount.  
- **Min/Max Quantity:** Restrict based on item count.  
- **Scope:** Apply to entire cart, specific products, or collections.  

---

## Step 4: User Eligibility

Control who can redeem the offer with **User Eligibility** settings.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/PurchaseDriven/image3.png"
  alt="User eligibility section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Audience Type:**  
  - *All users*  
  - *Specific users only*  
- **Order Scope:**  
  - *All orders*  
  - *First order only*  
  - *Based on total order count*  

---

## Step 5: Usage Limits

Manage how often the coupon can be used under **Usage Limits**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/PurchaseDriven/image4.png"
  alt="Usage limits section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Global Usage Limit:** Maximum number of total redemptions.  
- **Per-Customer Limit:** Restrict individual usage count.  
- **Stacking Permissions:** Enable or disable use with other discounts.  
- **Active Window:** Define start and end dates/times.  
- **Disable COD:** Optionally restrict Cash on Delivery when active.  

---

## Step 6: Preview

Refine how the offer appears to customers under **Preview**.

<img
  className="rounded-lg"

  alt="Preview section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Coupon Visibility:** Show or hide during checkout.

## Strategic Value

The **Purchase Driven Coupon** transforms behavioral data into targeted engagement by:  
- Rewarding loyal customers and repeat buyers.  
- Encouraging higher spending through milestone incentives.  
- Powering data-driven loyalty programs and retention campaigns.  

With precise behavioral filters and flexible setup, FlexyPe enables personalized, performance-focused promotions that strengthen customer relationships without margin erosion.
