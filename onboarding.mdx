## Collaborate Store with FlexyPe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing your Collaboration code from Shopify">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-1.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Login to your Shopify Admin account and go to Settings.
    - Click on Settings on the bottom left panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Getting your Collaboration code">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-2.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - Go to Users and Permissions and scroll down to Collaborators section.
    - Copy the Collaboration Code and share it along with your Store URL so we can send you a collaboration request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accepting the collaboration request">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-3.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />
    - You will receive a collaboration request from **FlexyPe Developers**.
    - Please accept the request to begin your onboarding process.
    <Warning>
        To assist with your Shopify store, we need a collaboration code to gain access. You can keep our access active for quicker support in the future. All access is used strictly for store-related assistance.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
