## Setup your FlexyPe

Follow the steps below to install and configure the FlexyPe app for your Shopify store.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share collaborator code with the FlexyPe team">
    Provide your Shopify store's collaborator request code to the FlexyPe team so we can request access to your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept collaborator request">
    After the FlexyPe team sends the request, accept it in your Shopify admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the FlexyPe app">
    Once access is granted, we’ll share an installation link for the FlexyPe app. Use the link to install the app in your Shopify store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete app setup">
    Open the app and complete the initial setup process:
    - Business Type
    - Business Details
    - Business Team
    - Payment Setup
    - Analytics Integration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize features to your needs">
    Explore the available app features and adjust settings as required to fit your business needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let us know your theme">
    Share the name of the theme where you'd like FlexyPe to be integrated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your testing link">
    Within 12 hours of theme confirmation, we’ll share a preview/testing link so you can verify the integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live with FlexyPe">
    Once everything looks good, publish your theme. Congratulations — you're now live with FlexyPe!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need help?

If you get stuck or need help with any of the above steps, don’t hesitate to reach out to our team at [hello@flexype.com](mailto:hello@flexype.com).
