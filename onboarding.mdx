## Collaborate Store with FlexyPe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accessing your Collaboration code from Shopify">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-1.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - Login to your Shopify Admin account.
    - Click on Settings on the bottom left panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Getting your Collaboration code">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-2.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - Go to Users and then click on Security and scroll down to Collaborators section.
    - Copy the **Collaboration Code** and share it along with your **Store URL** so we can send you a collaboration request.

    <Warning>
      Please do **not** click on “Generate New Code” after sharing the collaboration code until the FlexyPe checkout integration is fully completed.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accepting the collaboration request">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-3.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - You will receive a collaboration request from **FlexyPe Developers**.
    - Please accept the request to begin your onboarding process.

    <Note>
      As part of your onboarding, we’ll need a collaboration code to access your Shopify store. This allows us to assist more efficiently, both now and in the future. Our access is used solely for store-related support.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installation of FlexyPe app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant permissions and Install the app">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-4.png"

    />

## Complete Pre-Integration Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete payment gateway setup">
    <Note>
      If you didn’t configure the Payment Gateways right after installing the FlexyPe app and completing the initial setup, you can still do it later by logging into your FlexyPe dashboard.
    </Note>
    If you already have **active accounts** with the following Payment Gateways, please note the below:

    1. [**PayU**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/payu) – Credentials can be accessed from the PayU dashboard, and webhook setup is mandatory. Configuration happens instantly once done.
    2. [**Cashfree**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/cashfree) – Credentials are available in the Cashfree dashboard, and webhook setup is required. Configuration is completed instantly thereafter.
    3. [**XPay**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/xpay) – Credentials are available in the XPay dashboard, and webhook configuration is required. Setup is completed instantly after this.
    4. [**Easebuzz**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/easebuzz) – Credentials will be shared by the Easebuzz team, and webhook configuration is required. Once credentials are received, the PG configuration can be completed instantly.
    5. [**Razorpay**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/razorpay) – Account authorization is required and webhook setup is not needed. Setup is completed instantly after this.
    6. [**PhonePe**](https://docs.flexype.io/integrations/payment/phonepe) – MID needs to be shared to FlexyPe team. The mapping process is handled manually from our end and typically takes up to a week to complete.

    <Note>
      Click on the payment gateway name from the above list to view the complete configuration process.
    </Note>
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-7.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - Open the FlexyPe app from your Shopify Admin.
    - If you haven’t completed your payment gateway setup yet, visit [Payment Provider](/integrations/payment).
    - Setup is required before proceeding with the integration.
    - After completing the payment gateway setup, select the gateways you wish to enable for each **Payment Method**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect advertising tools">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-8.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - Connect Meta Pixel, Google Ads, and Google Analytics if you plan to run ads.
    - For easy step-by-step instructions, visit [Analytics](/onboarding).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore app features and settings">
    - Review the key features available within the app.
    - Adjust settings as needed to tailor the app to your business requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your theme for integration">
    <img
      className="rounded-lg"
      src="/images/onboarding/onboarding-9.png"
      alt="Hero Dark"
    />

    - Let us know which theme you'd like duplicated for the integration.
    - If no theme is specified, we will duplicate your live theme and proceed with the integration there.
  </Step>
</Steps>
